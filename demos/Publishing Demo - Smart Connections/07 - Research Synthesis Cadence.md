# Research Synthesis Cadence

A research synthesis cadence keeps the AI product team from drowning in isolated observations. The cadence should regularly convert interviews, support notes, eval findings, and launch retrospectives into a smaller set of product claims.

A good synthesis note names what the team now believes, what evidence supports it, what remains uncertain, and which decisions it should influence. This is especially important for AI systems because the product experience is shaped by both interface design and model behavior.

The cadence should feed the AI Ops Research Hub with durable summaries that connect customer needs, evaluation gaps, model choices, and launch decisions.
