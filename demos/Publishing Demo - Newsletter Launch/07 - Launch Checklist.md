# Launch Checklist

The launch checklist should include finishing the landing page, sending the beta-reader review request, loading the first four issues into the editor, testing the signup form, writing the welcome email, and preparing a short launch post.

Every checklist item should have an owner, a due date, and a fallback. The most important launch-day risk is not a technical failure; it is vague positioning. If the headline, welcome email, and first issue do not describe the same promise, subscribers will not know why they signed up.

The checklist should be reviewed against reader interview themes and beta feedback before anything goes public.
