# Welcome Email Draft

The welcome email should thank the reader, restate the promise, and tell them exactly what happens next. The tone should be direct and useful: every Tuesday they will receive one consulting system, one short example, and one action they can complete that week.

The email should ask one question: "What is the most annoying part of finding or keeping good consulting work right now?" Replies will become research for future issues and should be reviewed during the post-launch feedback check.

The draft should link back to landing-page messaging and beta-reader feedback so the launch voice stays consistent from signup through the first issue.
