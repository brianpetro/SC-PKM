# How I build context in my notes

My goal is to connect ideas across topics so that I can think in networks, not silos. Instead of manually linking everything, I let Smart Connections surface relevant notes that relate to what I am working on.

This reduces friction and helps me discover relationships I might have missed. Over time, these connections create a living knowledge base that compounds.

When a note feels complete, I review the suggested connections and link the ones that truly add value. This keeps my graph meaningful and my thinking clear.

The more I write, the better the suggestions become, turning my notes into a context-aware system that evolves with me.

While writing, I link related notes and surface connections inline. This keeps me in flow and helps new ideas land in the right context.
