# Customer Signal Triage

Customer signal triage turns qualitative feedback into product evidence. Support tickets, interview notes, sales calls, and community posts often describe the same product gap with different language. The triage process groups these signals by user intent, workflow stage, severity, and confidence.

In an AI product, customer signals are especially important because model behavior can change while the interface looks stable. A complaint about vague answers may be a prompt issue, an evaluation gap, or a mismatch between the product promise and the current model capability.

A strong triage note should capture the customer quote, the suspected system behavior, the affected workflow, and the decision it may influence. The AI Ops Research Hub should surface these triage notes beside evaluation protocols and launch decisions.
