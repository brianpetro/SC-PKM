> [!summary]
> Energy is a constraint. Track it lightly and schedule recovery before you crash.

> [!When to use]
> When you feel depleted, or once per week to prevent slow burnout.

### Failure mode and fix
Failure mode: You notice low energy but do not change the plan.

Fix: the check-in is incomplete until you schedule one recovery block.

### Covers / Does not cover
- Covers: Lightweight energy awareness + one adjustment.
- Does not cover: Medical advice.
