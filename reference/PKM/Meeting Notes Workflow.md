> [!summary]
> Capture meetings into decisions, actions, and context that stays linked to projects.

> [!When to use]
> If meetings generate notes but no outcomes, this note shows how to turn meetings into decisions and actions you can actually track, even in back-to-back calls.

### Meeting note template and capture rules
- Title: Meeting - Topic - YYYY-MM-DD
- Attendees:
- Purpose:

### Extract decisions, actions, and open questions
- Decisions: what was decided, and why.
- Actions: what someone will do next.
- Open questions: unresolved items with an owner.

### Link to project hubs and stakeholders
- Link to the project note (hub) at the top.
- Link to decision records you created.
- Link to relevant reference notes that explain context.

### Follow-up and review cadence
- End of day: add meeting note to [[Capturing Information]] if not already.
- Immediately after meeting: 2 minutes to extract actions and decisions.
- Weekly: during review, scan meeting actions and verify closure.

### Avoid recording everything, keep outcomes
- Failure mode: "I wrote a transcript and could not find decisions."
- Failure mode: "Actions disappeared."
- Failure mode: "Notes are unlinked so context is lost."


## Next action
Create a Meeting Notes template that always yields Decisions + Actions, and use it for your next meeting.
