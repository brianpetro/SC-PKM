> [!summary]
> Recovery is easier when it is scheduled and small.

> [!When to use]
> When energy is low, after heavy meeting days, or midweek to prevent accumulation.

### Template
```
# Recovery Block (30-60 min)

> [!summary]
> Treat recovery as a scheduled block so it actually happens.

## Menu (pick 1-2)
- [ ] Walk outside (no phone)
- [ ] Stretch or light workout
- [ ] Nap or quiet rest
- [ ] Journal: 5 bullets (wins, friction, one change)
- [ ] Process 10 inbox items (closure creates relief)
- [ ] Social recharge (one call or short meet)

## Exit checkpoint (2 minutes)
- Energy after (0-10):
- One thing I will do differently next:
```

### Failure mode and fix
Failure mode: You use recovery as avoidance.

Fix: keep it timeboxed and end with one next step. If you need rest, rest. If you need closure, process.

### Covers / Does not cover
- Covers: A practical recovery routine with an exit checkpoint.
- Does not cover: Long-term burnout root-cause analysis.

## Next action
- Schedule one recovery block this week and use this template.
