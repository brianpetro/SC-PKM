### Prompts
# Progressive Summarization Layer Prompts

> [!summary]
> Prompts for moving a source note from raw capture to skimmable and reusable.

## Layer 0 -> 1: "What is worth highlighting?"
Use when you just captured a source (article, book chapter, talk).
- What is the author's main claim (1 sentence)?
- What 3 examples or mechanisms support it?
- What is the one thing I would want to remember in 30 days?

Mark only:
- definitions
- claims
- examples
- constraints or caveats

## Layer 1 -> 2: "What are the few lines future-me needs?"
Use when you already have highlights but skimming is slow.
- Pick 5 lines max.
- Prefer lines that change a decision or enable an action.

## Layer 2 -> permanent notes: "What is the reusable idea?"
Use when a highlight repeats across sources.
- Write the claim in your own words.
- Add one concrete example from your context.
- Link it to 1-3 related notes with relationship sentences.

## Stop rule
If you cannot name the intended use, archive the source.

### Failure mode and fix
Failure mode: You over-highlight and create noise.

Fix: enforce the 5 key lines rule and stop when you can explain the idea without the source open.
