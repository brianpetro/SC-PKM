> [!summary]

> [!When to use]
> During monthly or quarterly reviews, or when a MOC feels stale or bloated.

### Checklist
# MOC Refresh Checklist

> [!summary]
> A 10-minute check to keep MOCs curated and current.

## Refresh steps
- [ ] Remove 3 stale or low-value links
- [ ] Add 1-2 new high-value links discovered recently
- [ ] Add 1 "Start here" link for beginners
- [ ] Add 1 "Next action" or "Gap" you want to fill
- [ ] Update "Last refreshed" date

## Optional: improve glue text
- For the top 5 links, add/refresh the one-sentence reason it belongs.

### Failure mode and fix
Failure mode: You never update MOCs.

Fix: schedule a 10-minute refresh in your monthly review and refresh only one MOC per month.

### Covers / Does not cover
- Covers: A lightweight maintenance routine for MOCs.
- Does not cover: Deep restructuring of your vault.
