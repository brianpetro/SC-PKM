> [!summary]
> A habit review is how habits improve; tracking alone is not enough.

> [!When to use]
> During a weekly review, when you want to improve consistency without overthinking.

### Prompt block
> [!summary]
> A 5-minute weekly reflection that improves adherence without guilt.

## Prompts
- What habit was easiest? Why?
- What habit slipped most? What friction caused it?
- What is the smallest change that would make next week easier?
- Did I choose the right habit level (too hard / too easy)?
- What is one reward I can attach to completion?

## Output
End with one concrete change:
- Change for next week:

### Failure mode and fix
Failure mode: You turn the review into a long journal.

Fix: answer in 5 bullets max and choose one change.

### Covers / Does not cover
- Covers: Weekly reflection prompts for habit improvement.
- Does not cover: Monthly goal setting.
