> [!summary]
> The inbox is a staging area, not storage.

> [!When to use]
> When captures pile up or you want a predictable process to turn notes into usable assets.

### Workflow
> [!summary]
> A short path from raw capture to a reusable note, without over-processing.

## Step 1: Capture (fast)
Include:
- What (the idea)
- Why (intended use)
- Source (optional)

## Step 2: Triage (daily or weekly)
Each item becomes one of:
- Delete (not worth keeping)
- Task (put where you execute)
- Source note (if citation/provenance matters)
- Permanent note (if reusable)

## Step 3: Convert (1-3 items)
For each promoted item:
- Rewrite in your own words
- Add 1 example
- Add 1-3 links with relationship sentences

## Step 4: Place
- Put durable notes in References
- Put active work in Projects
- Archive finished work

## Step 5: Close
End with exactly one next action.

### Failure mode and fix
Failure mode: You try to fully process everything.

Fix: process the best 1-3 items and delete or archive the rest.

### Covers / Does not cover
- Covers: A short conversion path from capture to reusable notes.
- Does not cover: Detailed note taxonomy decisions.
