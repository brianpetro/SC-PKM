> [!summary]
> Weekly reset to process inbox, connect ideas, and plan the next week.

> [!When to use]
> If weeks drift and loose ends accumulate, this weekly review resets the system so you start Monday clear, even after a chaotic week.

### Weekly checklist for review and cleanup
- 10 min: inbox triage.
- 15 min: project scan.
- 10 min: link + MOC updates.

### Project and area scan for alignment
- What is the next deliverable?
- What decision is pending?
- Who is waiting on me?

### Connect new notes and update MOCs
- Convert meeting notes into decisions and actions (see [[Processing and Organizing Notes]]).
- Add one relationship sentence to the most important new note.
- Update 1-2 MOCs or index notes so clusters are easy to find later.

### Plan next week actions and outputs
- 2-4 calendar blocks tied to those outcomes (see [[Time Management]]).
- 1-3 outcomes you want by end of week.
- A short "next actions" list per active project.

### Identify one improvement to the system
- Fix one naming convention issue you noticed repeatedly.
- Fix: end the review with a visible improvement or micro-output.
- Create one saved query you wish you had.
