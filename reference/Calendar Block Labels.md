> [!summary]
> If you cannot scan your calendar and know what matters, labels fix it.

> [!When to use]
> When your calendar is full but you cannot tell what the blocks are for.

### Label set
> [!summary]
> A tiny label system that makes your calendar scannable and honest.

## Recommended labels
- Deep: outcome work (writing, building, deciding)
- Meeting: coordination and conversations
- Admin: maintenance and small tasks
- Buffer: processing and recovery between contexts
- Recovery: rest, walk, reset

## Rules
- Name blocks by deliverable: "Deep - Draft section 2", not "Work time".
- Add buffers after meetings (10-30 min).
- Protect at least one Deep block per week.

### Failure mode and fix
Failure mode: You invent 20 labels.

Fix: keep 5 labels max and only add a new one if it improves retrieval or planning.

### Covers / Does not cover
- Covers: A minimal calendar label vocabulary.
- Does not cover: Full scheduling strategies (see [[Scheduling & Calendar Management]]).
