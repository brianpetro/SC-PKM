### Definition and promise of a second brain
- a trusted place where important knowledge lives outside your head,
- organized so you can retrieve it quickly,
- and maintained so it stays useful over time.

### Core practices (capture, organize, retrieve)
- [[Progressive Summarization]] makes sources skimmable so retrieval is fast, not a rereading project.
- Capture: collect ideas and inputs with intent.
- Organize: place them where they will be found later.

### Structure choices (PARA, MOCs, links)
- PARA for lifecycle and storage (projects, areas, references, archive).
- MOCs for navigation and curated maps.
- Links for meaning and relationships.

### Review and maintenance to keep it alive
- No maintenance cadence. Fix: schedule reviews and degrade them gracefully when busy.
- Daily: keep inbox small.
- Weekly: reset priorities and process backlog.
