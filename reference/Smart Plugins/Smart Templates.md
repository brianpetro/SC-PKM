> [!summary]
> Smart Templates helps you turn repeatable work (meeting notes, analyses, outlines) into predictable outputs by running templates that include prompts.

> [!tip] When to use
> Use this when you want the same kind of output repeatedly, with consistent structure and less blank-page friction.
