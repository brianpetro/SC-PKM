> [!summary]
> Habit tracking is a feedback loop, not a morality scorecard.

> [!When to use]
> When you want consistency and you need visibility into what is actually happening.

### Tracking rules that prevent overwhelm
- Track 1-3 habits.
- Track daily, review weekly.
- Measure the minimum viable behavior (not the perfect version).

### A simple tracker format
Use checkboxes in a weekly note, or a single table.

See [[Habit Tracker]] for a copy/paste version.

### Weekly review questions
- What habit is slipping and why?
- What friction showed up?
- What is the smallest change that would improve adherence?

### Failure mode and fix
Failure mode: You track too many things and stop.

Fix: delete 80% of tracked items and keep only the keystone (see [[Keystone Habits]]).

### Covers / Does not cover
- Covers: A lightweight way to track and review habits.
- Does not cover: Complex quantified-self metrics.
