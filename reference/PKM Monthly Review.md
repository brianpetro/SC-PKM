> [!summary]
> Monthly zoom-out to synthesize themes, realign goals, and clean up accumulated notes.

> [!When to use]
> If you lose the big picture, this monthly review helps you synthesize themes and reset priorities, without needing a full overhaul.

### Monthly questions to ask and answer
- What did I ship or finish this month?
- What kept showing up (themes, problems, wins)?
- What do I want next month to be about?

### Theme and pattern extraction from notes
- Theme extraction is the monthly skill.
- Lightweight method: 1) Pull the last 20-50 meaningful notes you touched.
- 2) Write 3-5 themes as short phrases.

### Cleanup tasks (rename, merge, archive)
- Fix metadata drift (tags, aliases) in a small area.
- Rename 3 notes whose titles block retrieval.
- Merge or split 2 notes that violate atomicity.

### Goal alignment and next-month priorities
- Fix: translate one monthly priority into weekly outcomes during your next [[PKM Weekly Review]].
- Choose 1-2 priorities for next month (see [[Focus on Relevance]]).
- Identify the projects or areas they imply.

### Create one synthesis artifact or dashboard
- "Month in review" note with 5 bullet learnings + links.
- A refreshed MOC for your main domain.
- A decision record: "What I will change next month and why."
