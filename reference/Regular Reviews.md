> [!summary]
> Schedule daily/weekly/monthly reviews so notes stay current and the system stays trusted.

> [!When to use]
> If your system slowly degrades, this note helps you pick a review cadence that keeps it trusted, even when life gets busy.

### Why cadence prevents entropy
- forcing regular processing,
- refreshing context,
- and pruning what no longer matters.

### Daily review minimal checklist
- Process a few inbox items.
- Extract next actions.
- Add 1-3 links to important new notes.

### Weekly review reset checklist
- Weekly review becomes a 3-hour audit. Fix: timebox it and prioritize clearing backlog + choosing next actions.
- Empty or reduce inbox to a small number.
- Review active projects and update hub notes.

### Monthly review synthesis checklist
- Archive finished work and stale captures.
- Upgrade 1-3 notes into evergreen assets.
- Consolidate tags (remove one-off tags).

### How to adjust cadence when busy
- Reviews also support [[Future Proofing]] by keeping the system healthy and preventing long-term rot.
- Keep daily review but shrink it to 3 minutes.
- Keep weekly review but shrink to 20 minutes and focus on inbox + priorities.
