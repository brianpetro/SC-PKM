> [!summary]
> Email becomes manageable when it has a schedule and rules.

> [!When to use]
> When you check email constantly or your inbox never feels under control.

### Workflow
> [!summary]
> Two processing windows per day keeps email from running your day.

## The loop
- Block 1 (morning): 15-30 min triage
- Block 2 (late afternoon): 15-30 min clear and close loops

## 4D triage (each message gets one)
- Delete: not needed
- Delegate: forward with owner + ask
- Defer: schedule or add to task list with a due date
- Do: if it takes <2 minutes, do it now

## Processing rules
- Do not reread email all day.
- Unsubscribe aggressively from low-value sources.
- If an email implies a decision, capture it in [[Decision Logs]] (briefly).

## Small win metric
- End-of-day inbox count (trend down)

### Failure mode and fix
Failure mode: You keep the inbox open outside the blocks.

Fix: close the tab/app between blocks. If you must check, do it at the top of the hour and keep it under 2 minutes.

### Covers / Does not cover
- Covers: A daily email processing rhythm and triage rule.
- Does not cover: Advanced email filtering setups.

## Next action
- Pick two times today for inbox processing and close your email outside those windows.
