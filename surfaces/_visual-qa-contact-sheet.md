# Surface visual QA contact sheets

> [!summary]
> Use these contact sheets to review screenshots by surface before choosing publication candidates or recapture targets.

## WHY

A surface note proves ownership and capture method. A contact sheet proves whether the visual set actually works when scanned as a portfolio. The audience should see PKM, retrieval, linking, synthesis, and Obsidian-native workflows before they see abstract UI chrome.

## WDLL

```md
A reviewer can open one workflow note, jump to native or plugin contact sheets, scan all indexed images, and mark weak screenshots for recapture without hunting across campaigns or archives.
```

## QA checks

- [ ] Surface identity is clear without reading the filename.
- [ ] Screenshot content appeals to PKM users: capture, connect, retrieve, synthesize, review, or publish knowledge.
- [ ] Disabled native plugins show truthful disabled state instead of fake usage.
- [ ] No account email, private note content, publish destination, microphone prompt, or web browsing side effect is visible.
- [ ] Plugin screenshots remain grouped under their owning surface note.
