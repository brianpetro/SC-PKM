# FAQ and Objections

The FAQ should answer skeptical questions before launch: Is this only for new consultants? Is it about marketing or operations? How long does each issue take to read? Will there be templates? What happens after signup?

The best answer is specific: the newsletter is for consultants who already have skills but want a steadier system for choosing offers, following up, and learning from their client work. Each issue should take less than ten minutes to read and should include one action.

Objections should be pulled from beta-reader feedback and landing-page review notes. If the same question appears three times, it belongs on the page or in the welcome email.
